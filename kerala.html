Kerala Backwaters

About the Region

Kerala's backwaters are a network of interconnected canals, rivers, lakes, and inlets formed by more than 900 km of waterways. These waterways have long been used for transportation, fishing, and agriculture.

Popular Destinations

  • Alleppey: Known as the Venice of the East, famous for houseboat cruises
  • Kumarakom: Bird sanctuary and luxury resorts
  • Munnar: Tea plantations and misty mountains

Best Time to Visit

  • October to February (Peak Season)
  • March to May (Summer)
  • June to September (Monsoon)

Activities

  • Houseboat Stays
  • Ayurvedic Treatments
  • Village Tours
  • Traditional Dance Shows
  • Kerala Cuisine Experience
